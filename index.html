Maintenance services to keep your garden perfect all year round

At Lucky Shrub, we understand that a perfect garden is a well-maintained garden. Our comprehensive maintenance services ensure your green space thrives in every season. From the vibrant blooms of spring to the serene beauty of winter, we've got you covered. Our offerings include:


  • Seasonal Pruning - Keeping your plants healthy and in shape.
  • Lawn Care - Regular mowing, aerating, and fertilizing for a lush lawn.
  • Pest and Disease Management - Proactive treatments to protect your garden.
  • Irrigation System Maintenance - Ensuring efficient water use and plant hydration.
  • Mulching - To retain soil moisture and suppress weeds.

Let Lucky Shrub's expert team handle the details, so you can enjoy a pristine garden, no matter the season.
